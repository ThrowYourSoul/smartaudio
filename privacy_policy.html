Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Smart Audio Extractor ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android application.

1. Local Processing

All audio extraction, conversion, and trimming operations are performed entirely on your device. Your media files are never uploaded to any server. We do not have access to your files.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The App may collect the following non-personal data through third-party services:

  • Usage analytics: Anonymous app usage data (screens visited, features used) via Firebase Analytics to improve the App.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash and error reports via Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs.
  • Advertising data: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Storage / Media access: To read video and audio files you select, and to save converted audio files to your device.
  • Notifications: To show audio playback controls and conversion progress in the notification bar.

We only use these permissions for the stated purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

5. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your media files remain under your control. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your files.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
